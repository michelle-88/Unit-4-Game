Instructions

Help the Avengers collect the Infinity Stones before Thanos!

At the start of the game, you will be given a random target number and 4 infinity stones. By clicking on a stone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number, and you will lose if your total score goes above the target number.
